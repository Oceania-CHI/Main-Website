2026 Australia & New Zealand @ CHI

The Oceania CHI Event brings together researchers and practitioners from across Australia, Aotearoa New Zealand, and the Pacific HCI community. Hosted alongside CHI 2026 in Barcelona, the event celebrates the region’s research contributions while creating opportunities for connection and new collaborations. This website highlights participating labs, publications, and event information for this year’s gathering.

Publications

This section will highlight publications from the Oceania HCI community accepted to CHI 2026. Once the final program is released, we will curate a list of papers, late-breaking works, workshops, and other contributions from researchers across Australia, Aotearoa New Zealand, and the Pacific. A booklet featuring highlights from these publications will also be available for download.

Event

On Tuesday evening, 14 April, we invite you to join us for the inaugural Australia and New Zealand HCI Networking Event at CHI 2026 in Barcelona. The event will bring together researchers, practitioners, and students from across Australia, Aotearoa New Zealand, and the Pacific HCI community to connect, celebrate our region’s contributions, and spark new collaborations. We are excited to begin this tradition and look forward to many gatherings to come.

OzCHI 2026

The 38th Australian Conference on Human–Computer Interaction (OzCHI 2026) will take place in Adelaide, Australia from 21–25 November 2026. OzCHI is the premier conference for Human–Computer Interaction research in the Australasian region, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and students to share new ideas and advances in the field.
